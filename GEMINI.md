# OpenGL Flight Simulator
It's a c++ & opengl-based ambitious project to utilize real-time aerodynamic simulation, realistic volumetric cloud simulation.
The player can:
1. Enjoy flying with realistic aerodynamic simulation across varying weather.
2. Customize design their plane with provided basic items (or import their own 3D models).
3. Enjoy the beauty of flying between beautifle clouds, daytime.
4. Enjoy the condition of realistic crashing physics.
